We would like to schedule a donation appointment at your convenience. By setting up an appointment, you can choose a date and time that works best for you. Your Apointment has the potential to create a lasting difference in the lives of those we serve.

Steps to Make Appointment:

Click Make Appointment.
Choose the available date and time for your Donations.
You can ask appointment only to donate Food ,Clothes and Stationary Products (Example: Notebooks, Pen, Pencil for Students and Wheelchairs, Bed for Hospitals).
By filling all the information you can request an appointment.
An appointment request will send to Charity Miles.You will receive an accepted or cancelled status from the Charity.
